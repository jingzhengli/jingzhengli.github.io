About me

I am currently a Assistant Researcher, Zhongguancun laboratory, collaborated with Prof. Xianglong Liu. I received Ph.D. from the ACT Lab, Beihang University(BUAA), under the supervision of Prof. Wenfei Fan and Dr. Hailong Sun (co-supervisor).

Recently, I have been working on the safety of autonomous driving using simulation, reinforcement learning, and adversarial attacks, as well as safety-critical scenarios generation.
I focus on robust deep learning under distribution shift. My main research interests include Multimodal AI, Vision and Language, Transfer Learning, Self supervised/Semi-supervised/Weakly-supervised Learning.

Reasearch Projects

  1. Artificial Intelligence Safety for Autonomous Driving.

  2. Research on Quality Modeling and Assessment Technology of Crowd Intelligent System in Complex Task Processing.
