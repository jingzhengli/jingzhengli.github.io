About me

I am currently a Assistant Researcher, Zhongguancun laboratory, collaborated with Prof. Xianglong Liu.

I focus on robust deep learning under distribution shift. My main research interests include Multimodal AI, Vision and Language, Transfer Learning, Self supervised/Semi-supervised/Weakly-supervised Learning.
Recently, I have been working on the safety of autonomous driving using simulation, reinforcement learning, and adversarial attacks, as well as safety-critical scenarios generation.
